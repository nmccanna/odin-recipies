Quesadilla

Quesadillas are a very popular Mexican dish. You can find these at just about any Mexican resturant. Quesadillas taste best when cooked on an open pan or on a stove.

Choosing the right ingredients is very important. A good tortila with good meat and cheese can go a long way. Quesadillas are most commomly eaten with chicken, beef, or pork. A good blend of Mexican style shreaded cheese and a good toasty tortila will make your Quesadilla taste delicious. Add a touch of sour cream on the side for some added flavor.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Chicken, Beef, or Pork
  • Mexican blend of shreaded cheese

Steps

  1. Lay tortila on pan
  2. Place shreaded cheese on top
  3. Place your meat of choice on top
  4. Cook until cheese is melted (but not so much that you burn the tortila underneath
  5. Fold torilla over in half and serve on plate
  6. Add a cool scoop of sour cream on the side if you want
