Lasagna

Lasagna is an Italian recipe which is popular to eat all over the world. You can find this dish at just about any Italian resturant. It's also a great dish to cook at home for yourself or to impress your friends and family.

Lasagna is made up of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It is cooked in an oven and ready to eat. Just wait a few minutes for it to cool off. It's good to eat with a glass of wine or a can of your favorite soda. It's a good meal for any occasion. Don't forget to eat it with a warm slice of cheesy garlic bread too! Mama mia! Muuuahh!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps

  1. Place down slice of pasta
  2. Put meat on it
  3. Put cheese on top of the meat
  4. Put tomato sauce on top of the cheese
  5. Put a slice of pasta on top of the cheese
  6. Repeat steps
