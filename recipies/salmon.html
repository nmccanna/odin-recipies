Salmon

Salmon is a delicious dish to eat if you pick the right fish and it's cooked properly. I prefer atlantic salmon instead of sockeye salmon because sockeye salmon has bones in it.

Salmon tastes great with conola oil and your favorite flavor of fish seasoning on it. It tastes best when cooked in an oven, a grill, or on an open fire outside. Add mashed potatoes with butter and a glass of your favorite soda or a cool glass of milk and you have the perfect meal.

Ingredients

  • Baking pan with aluminum foil
  • 1 pound of Atlantic salmon
  • Canola oil
  • Seasoning
  • Mashed potatoes
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees farenheight
  2. Place aluminum foil on baking pan
  3. Place salmon on aluminum foil
  4. Cover salmon with a thin coating of canola oil
  5. Sprinkle your favorite seasoning on top of it
  6. After oven is preheated, place in oven and set it for 30 mins
  7. Cook mashed potatoes and then add butter to it
